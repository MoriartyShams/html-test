South Park

Description

South Park is a long-running American animated sitcom known for its adult themes, satirical humor, and controversial portrayal of current events, pop culture, and political figures. The show follows the lives of four 10-year-old boys, Stan, Kyle, Kenny, and Eric, and their adventures in the fictional Colorado town of South Park.

Gin Tama

Description

Gintama is a Japanese manga and anime series by Hideaki Sorachi, set in an alternate Edo-era Japan where aliens have invaded and taken over the Shogunate. It features an eccentric samurai, Gintoki Sakata, and his group of friends, who work as freelancers, taking on various jobs to make ends meet.

Vigilante

Description

"Vigilante" is a South Korean action-thriller drama centered around Kim Ji-yong, a student at the Korean National Police University who takes on the role of a vigilante at night, punishing criminals who evade the law. He's driven by a desire for justice and his actions become a social phenomenon, attracting the attention of both the public and the police. .

Grimm

Description

"Grimm" is a dark fantasy police procedural drama that follows Nick Burkhardt, a Portland homicide detective, who discovers he is a Grimm, a descendant of a lineage tasked with protecting humanity from mythological creatures, also known as Wesen. The show explores the balance between humanity and the supernatural, featuring a world inspired by Grimm's fairy tales.

Oldboy

Description

"Oldboy" is a 2003 South Korean action-thriller film directed by Park Chan-wook, based on the Japanese manga of the same name. The story centers around Oh Dae-su, who is inexplicably kidnapped and imprisoned for 15 years before being released. After his release, he embarks on a mission to find his captor, driven by a desire for revenge.

Gone with the wind

Description

Gone with the Wind is an American historical novel by novelist Margaret Mitchell. The plot follows Scarlett O'Hara in the antebellum South, American Civil War, and Reconstruction periods as she negotiates a turbulent love life. Throughout the novel, Scarlett is fixated on Ashley Wilkes.
